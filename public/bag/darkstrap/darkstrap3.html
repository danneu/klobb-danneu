Darkstrap

Made by danneu


A dark theme for Twitter Bootstrap.

Usage

  1. Read the project's readme.
  2. Download darkstrap.css
  3. Include it after bootstrap.css

<head>
  <link href='stylesheets/bootstrap.css' rel='stylesheet' />
  <link href='stylesheets/darkstrap.css' rel='stylesheet' />
</head>
